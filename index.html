About B★-Trees

A B★-Tree (B-Star Tree) is a self-balancing tree data structure that maintains sorted data and allows searches, sequential access, insertions, and deletions in logarithmic time.

Unlike regular B-Trees, B★-Trees ensure that all nodes (except the root) are at least 2/3 full, providing better space utilization and performance.

In our library system, Book IDs serve as keys to efficiently organize and retrieve book records.
